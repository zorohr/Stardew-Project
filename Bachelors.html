We Are The Bachelors!

They can be married after 10 hearts together, choose wisely!

Oh Alex. The jock with dreams of going pro.
Find him at 1 River Road with his grandmother Evelyn,
grandfather George and Dusty the dog.
Loved gifts include: Complete Breakfast, the book "Jack Be Nimble,
Jack Be Thick" and Salmon Dinner.
He gets his cake on Summer 13.

Maybe he's born with it, maybe he's Elliott!
The local writer who will win your heart with his poetic dialogues.
This melodramatic man lives in a seaside cabin
where he can be inspired at any time!
Loved gifts include: Crab Cakes, Duck Feather, Lobster,
Pomegranates, Squid Ink and Tom Kha Soup!
This Libra celebrates his birthday on Fall 5.

Harvey is the town doctor
with a secret love of aerobics and model planes.
Find him in his apartment above the Clinic.
Loved gifts include: Coffee, Pickles, Super Meal,
Truffle Oil and Wine.
His birthday is Winter 14.

Meet Sam. He's a skater who plays guitar and likes video games.
He lives at 1 Willow Lane with his mother Jodi,
father Kent and his younger brother Vincent.
Loved gifts include: Cactus Fruit, Maple Bars, Pizza and Tigerseye.
Fall 11 is his birthday.

This is Sebastian! A freelance programmer
who loves frogs and working on his motorcycle.
He lives at 24 Mountain Road with his mother Robin,
half-sister Maru and his stepdad Demetrius.
Loved gifts include: Frog and Void Eggs, Obsidian,
Frozen Tear, Sashimi and Pumpkin Soup!
Don't forget his birthday on Winter 10!

Say hello to Shane. He is a grumpy drinker
with a love of chickens.
He rents a room at his aunt Marnie's Ranch
with his Goddaughter Jas.
Loved gifts include: Beer, Hot Peppers,
Pepper Poppers and Pizza!
He was born on Spring 20.
